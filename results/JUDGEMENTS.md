- All scored attempts had `grade.json`; no hidden-test result had to be inferred manually.

## Bottom line

OpenCode + free was viable on the simple work in this pilot and matched Claude Code on Tiers 1-2, but it fell behind at Tier 3 and failed Tier 4. Paying for DeepSeek chat did not change the pass/fail answer in this run: it matched the free OpenCode arm exactly overall and by tier. The lines crossed at Tier 3: Claude stayed at 100%, while both OpenCode arms dropped to 66.7%, then both failed the Tier 4 debugging task.
